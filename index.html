Liberty Hair Design

Welcome to Liberty Hair Design - we offer a brilliant hair cutting and styling experience located in Holt, North Norfolk.

Our service is backed by passion and a we consider every clients' wishes, delivering to a large customer base of all ages.

Visit us at our beautiful Salon for a high quality offering from one of our talented stylists.

With many years experience, our team of stylists can give you the exact cut you are looking for, and offer advice about what would look great for you.

From re-touches to a full head tint. Give yourself a new, amazing colour to change your look from our talented stylists.

Get stylish with help from our amazing hair designers. We will get you looking your absolute best with a style that compliments you.
